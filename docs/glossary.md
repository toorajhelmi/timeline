## Glossary

- **Timeline**: a curated, time-ordered representation of a real-world topic/event across months/years.
- **Entry**: a timestamped (or time-ranged) unit added to a timeline.
- **Key moment**: an entry (or cluster) promoted for visibility at wider zoom levels.
- **Aggregation**: grouping many entries into a summary bucket at a given zoom level.
- **Correction**: an entry intended to supersede/refine a previous entry while preserving history.
- **Disputed**: a state indicating credible contradiction or unresolved conflict around an entry.
- **Provenance**: metadata describing where information came from (source, author, time, edits).
